About the K-12 Data Solutions "Protect My District" Project

Rielle Grant is the founder and CEO of K-12 Data Solutions.

K-12 Data Solutions is focused on helping the public education sector enhance their data privacy and protect themselves against cyber threats and data breaches.

The objective of Protect My District is to leverage extensive institutional data available to conduct a thorough analysis of cyber attacks within the K-12 education sector.

My individual contribution to the project is to create a sample website that utilizes complex data visualization tools to uncover the occurrences of cyber incidents in K-12 schools across the nation.
